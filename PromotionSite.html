Faculty Promotion Process

Lindsey Wilson University Promotion Criteria and Timeline

Overview

Authority: Promotion in rank shall be awarded by the Lindsey Wilson University Board of Trustees based upon the application by the faculty member to the Promotion Committee and subsequent recommendations from the Promotion Committee, VPAA, and president.

Evaluation Categories

  • Formal Letter of Application & CV
  • Three Letters of Recommendation
  • Teaching and mentoring
  • Service to the academic program, school, and college
  • Scholarship
    • Important: The higher the faculty rank, the greater the expectations. Additionally, there are commitment to excellence expectations defined in section 4 of the handbook for each rank in each category.

    Promotion Timeline

    August 22

    Promotion Training

    Representatives from the Promotion Committee and Faculty Affairs Council conduct training during the August Faculty Retreat to orient potential applicants and review process requirements. Open to all faculty members.

    September 15

    Application Submission

    Submit electronically to promotion@lindsey.edu:

    • Letter of Intent (including rank applying for, minimum criteria met, transcript confirmation, portfolio due date acknowledgment)
    • Curriculum vitae

    Deadline: 4:30 p.m. Central time

    September 30

    Eligibility Notification

    Promotion Committee chair notifies applicant of eligibility status after reviewing documentation with VPAA. Status will be either "meets requirements" or "does not meet requirements" with disqualifying details if applicable.

    January 24

    Recommendation Letter Status

    One week prior to deadline, Promotion Committee chair sends email regarding current status of letters of recommendation received and confirms once all three letters are received.

    January 31

    Portfolio & Letters Due

    Two key submissions required:

    • Three letters of recommendation (confidential, signed within past 6 months)
    • Electronic promotion portfolio using approved Google Sites template (max 100 MB)
    Note: After submission, applicant will never again have access to the portfolio and it will not be returned. Maintain personal copies for future reference.
    February 7

    Receipt Confirmation

    Promotion Committee chair sends email confirmation of portfolio receipt within one week. This acknowledgment does not provide feedback on completeness or quality.

    March 7

    Committee Review Complete

    Promotion Committee completes review using Promotion Portfolio Evaluation Rubric and submits scores, comments, and recommendation to VPAA. Chair notifies applicant of completion but does not reveal recommendation.

    March 15

    Administrative Review

    VPAA forwards portfolio and Committee recommendations with their own recommendation to president. President forwards all materials with their recommendation to Board of Trustees.

    Spring Board Meeting

    Final Decision

    Board of Trustees reviews all materials and recommendations to either award or deny promotion. Applicants notified in writing within one week of the meeting.

    May 15

    Feedback Provided

    Applicants receive:

    • Promotion Portfolio Evaluation Rubric scores (without reviewer names)
    • Written summary of comments (without reviewer names)
    • Written summary of all recommendations indicating "supportive" or "not supportive"

    Rank Criteria

    Instructor

    There is an expectation that instructors will complete the requirements to move to assistant professor rank.

    Assistant Professor

    Minimum criteria required by September 15

    • Earned doctorate/terminal degree (Instructors move to assistant professor upon completion), OR
    • Master's degree; AND
    • Minimum of three years of full-time college teaching experience completed at LWU with the rank of instructor

    Associate Professor

    Minimum criteria required by September 15

    • Earned doctorate/terminal degree; AND
    • Minimum of five years of full-time college teaching completed with a rank of assistant professor; AND
    • Minimum of three years of full-time college teaching experience completed at LWU

    Professor

    Minimum criteria required by September 15

    • Earned doctorate/terminal degree; AND
    • Minimum of ten years of full-time college teaching experience completed; AND
    • Minimum of seven years of full-time teaching experience completed with the rank of associate professor; AND
    • Minimum of five years of full-time college teaching experience completed at LWU

    Letter of Recommendation Requirements

    Three letters of recommendation required by January 31:

    Letter Requirements

    • Must be signed within the past six months
    • Must be received by January 31
    • Letters are confidential and shared only with Promotion Committee members, VPAA, President, and Board of Trustees

    Required Letter Sources

    Letter 1: Must be from the applicant's dean, director of Counseling (M.Ed.), director of HS, or RAD. (Deans applying for promotion must submit a letter from another school's dean.)

    Letters 2 & 3: Must be written by full-time Lindsey Wilson University faculty (not staff or adjunct instructors).

    Portfolio Submission: Electronic promotion portfolio must be submitted using the approved Google Sites template, shared with promotion@lindsey.edu, and must not exceed 100 MB.

    Salary Increases

    Promotion takes effect the next contract year immediately following the application year. A revised contract awarding a change in rank will be issued before the beginning of the fall term.

    Instructor to Assistant Professor

    Up to $2,000 increase

    Assistant Professor to Associate Professor

    Up to $3,500 increase

    Associate Professor to Professor

    Up to $5,000 increase
    Reapplication: Unsuccessful applicants for promotion may reapply the subsequent year, abiding by the guidelines and requirements stated in that subsequent year's Faculty Handbook.
